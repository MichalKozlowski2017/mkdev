**Last updated:** April 2026

## Data Controller

The data controller for personal data processed through this website, where applicable,
is the owner of **mkdev**. Detailed identity and privacy contact details can be provided
upon request through the contact channel listed with published apps.

## Data and Purpose

This website is **informational**: it lists apps and related documents. By default,
it does **not** run a user account system or custom data collection forms.

**Hosting and server logs:** the hosting provider may automatically process technical
data (for example IP address, timestamp, request ID) in server logs for security and
service reliability, based on legitimate interest where applicable under law.

**Cookies:** if future updates introduce cookies or similar mechanisms beyond strictly
necessary website operation, this policy will be updated with categories, retention,
and legal basis.

## Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights including access, rectification,
erasure, restriction, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

For privacy matters related to **this website**, use the same contact channels as for
app support (as listed in app store pages), with the note: `privacy / mkdev website`.

## Mobile Apps

**Each app has its own privacy policy** (where user data is processed), published under
its own URL and linked from the app page on this website. App policies do not replace this
website policy.
