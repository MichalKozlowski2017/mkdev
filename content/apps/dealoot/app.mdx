Dealoot is a mobile app (Android / iOS) that aggregates game discounts and free offers
from publicly available APIs.

## Core features

- **Hot Deals**: current discounts from multiple stores.
- **Free Games**: active giveaways and limited-time free offers.
- **Search**: title search with price comparison.
- **Price Alerts**: watchlist with target price tracking.

## Data sources

The app uses public APIs (including CheapShark, GamerPower, and IsThereAnyDeal)
to display offers.
